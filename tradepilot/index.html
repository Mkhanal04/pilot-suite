Welcome to TradePilot
Decision intelligence for supply chain.
Not an ERP - a decision layer.
ERPs manage transactions. TradePilot manages decisions. When a tariff changes, a supplier misses a deadline, or a port disruption cascades through your supply chain, TradePilot's 5 AI agents surface the risk, quantify the impact, and recommend action - with full transparency into why.
The 10-80-10 framework
Humans set thresholds and strategy (first 10%). 5 AI agents monitor, analyze, and recommend (the 80%). Humans review evidence and approve or override (final 10%). Every decision is traceable, auditable, and explainable.
