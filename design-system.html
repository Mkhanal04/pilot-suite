How I Work Section — Same card pattern
01 Discover with Software
Instead of months of requirements docs and wireframes, build a working prototype during discovery. Stakeholders react to real software, not slide decks. Hard questions surface in weeks, not quarters.
02 Design for Adoption
Apply HCAI principles from day one. Humans stay in control. AI decisions are transparent. Trust is designed into the product, not patched in after launch.
03 Fail Fast, Fail Cheap
If the product hypothesis is wrong, you find out before the engineering budget is committed. If it's right, you hand off a production-grade prototype with validated requirements.
