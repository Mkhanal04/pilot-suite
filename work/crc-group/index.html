Role

CRC Group

Product Strategy & Delivery Lead on a healthcare billing modernization program and the migration of two AI-enabled insurance platforms.

Apr 2025 — Present Dallas, TX via Synergech

Context

CRC Group runs $900M+ in healthcare premium volume. Two parallel tracks ran across my time there. The first was a billing modernization program (Goldengate) that needed to ship clean enough to support future M&A. The second is the current migration of two AI-enabled insurance platforms (Incepty + ClaimZilla) onto the CRC enterprise stack.

What I did

Goldengate · Apr – Oct 2025
Billing modernization, M&A-ready architecture
  • Cut 3+ months from the discovery-to-build timeline by running prototype-driven discovery directly with billing stakeholders, instead of the usual waterfall spec cycle.
  • Designed an M&A-ready billing architecture that supported $2M in cash-flow optimization through cleaner reconciliation workflows.
  • Project re-prioritized to 2027; transitioned to the Incepty / ClaimZilla migration.
Incepty + ClaimZilla · Nov 2025 — Present
Migration planning for two AI-enabled insurance platforms
  • Leading discovery and migration planning for two AI-enabled insurance platforms (submission intake + claims workflows) moving from Euclid to the CRC enterprise stack.
  • Decomposing undocumented workflows across Salesforce, Microsoft AI Foundry, SharePoint, PHP/Azure, KYC services, and APIM.
  • Mapping system interactions to surface migration risk; aligning business stakeholders with engineering leads on scope and sequencing.

Outcomes

  • 3+ months saved on discovery-to-build timeline via prototype-driven discovery.
  • $2M cash-flow optimization supported by the billing architecture design.
  • Two AI platform migrations currently in discovery and planning: Incepty (submission intake) and ClaimZilla (claims workflows).

Stack
