Role

World Kinect Services

Sr. Business Analyst across the Land and Aviation fuel divisions, driving 80% logistics process automation and the Oracle 11i replacement program through a multi-year modernization.

May 2016 — Nov 2021 Miami, FL 5+ years

Context

World Kinect is a global fuel and logistics operator headquartered in Miami, moving billions of gallons a year across commercial land fleets and aviation customers in dozens of countries. The Land and Aviation divisions run very different operations (truck and depot logistics on one side, airport and FBO fueling on the other) against a shared ERP backbone and a common finance and compliance stack. The role sat between those divisional operations teams, finance, and IT through a multi-year modernization program that included the Oracle 11i replacement and the organization's Waterfall-to-Agile transition.

What I did

  • Drove logistics process automation across both Land and Aviation divisions.
  • Led the Oracle 11i replacement program from requirements gathering through go-live.
  • Acted as primary liaison between operations, finance, and IT during the organization's Waterfall-to-Agile transition.
  • Supported cross-divisional process harmonization across Land and Aviation workflows.
  • Grew from first enterprise role into a senior BA managing multi-year programs end to end.

Outcomes

  • 80% automation of logistics processes across Land and Aviation divisions.
  • Oracle 11i replacement shipped to production.
  • Waterfall-to-Agile transition adopted across the BA function during tenure.
