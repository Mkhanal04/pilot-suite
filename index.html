The Thesis

Cross-domain pattern recognition as a product skill.

"I see what connects industries before the market does, and I build the proof."

Most product managers go deep in one vertical. I go wide across verticals and find the transferable architecture. Supply chain and recruiting look nothing alike — until you see that both need AI agents monitoring data streams, humans making high-stakes decisions with full transparency, and organizations acting on intelligence they didn't know they had. The architecture is the product. The domain is just a variable.
